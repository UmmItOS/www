Welcome to the official UmmItOS documentation. This site serves as the central reference for installing, configuring, and customizing UmmItOS — a streamlined Arch Linux distribution built around the Hyprland dynamic window manager.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Installation** — Step-by-step guides covering prerequisites, automated and manual installation methods, and post-install setup.
- **Configuration** — Detailed references for customizing your environment, including keybindings, screenshot tools, and system information displays.
- **Packages** — A complete overview of the default packages shipped with UmmItOS.

<Callout type="info">This documentation is under active development and will continue to evolve as the project matures. Contributions and feedback are welcome.</Callout>
